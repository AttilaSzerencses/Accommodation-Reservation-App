Why are we different from an average accommodation booking site?

  • - We understand the importance of convenience and ease when it comes to booking a hotel room, which is why we offer an automatic system that eliminates the need for a reception.
  • - With our system, customers can receive their room key through a QR code sent via email or through their own personal code. This means you can check in and access your room without the need for any assistance from hotel staff.
  • - We also take the safety and security of our customers' information very seriously, which is why we collect bank card data upon booking. This ensures that if a customer fails to return their room key on time, we can charge their card accordingly.
